About Balora

Volleyball your way - Anytime, Anywhere.

Brand Vision

In a world where international students struggle to commit to full-time sports teams due to busy schedules, we believe that creating a flexible platform for finding casual, fun volleyball matches will allow people to stay active, socialize, and enjoy the sport without the pressure of long-term commitment. Only then can we create a sports community where people can connect and play on their own terms.

Our Values

Balora brings people together through a shared love for volleybal. We create a place where everyone feels welcomed.

Everyone is invited, regardless of their skill-level, language, goal, or background. Balora celebrates everyone, from their nationality to their gender - everyone is welcome at Balora.

Volleyball is nothing without a team, and sometimes, you just need that extra help to find your team. That's where Balora comes in. We offer a collaborative space where we create a strong community by allowing you to organize and play together.

Balora offers a way to turn strangers into teammates, and teammates into friends. We create that lasting, memorable, spontaneous and connection through volleyball.

Finding teammates for a volleyball match should be quick and easy. Balora makes this concept accessible and within reach for everyone.

Volleyball is about having fun, and so are we. Balora brings humour, spontaneity, and energy into your volleyball experience.
Logo

Our logo gives off a warm, welcoming, and energetic feel — just like a great team spirit. This emphasizes our brand value of connection and accessibility. With the open hands, the large cartoony eyes and the encouraging smile, the logo communicates a friendly and approachable style, making everyone feel included and encouraged to join in – pointing out that we value inclusivity and community.
We’re all about building connections and being memorable, so our logo is engaging, uplifting, and stands out due to it's playfulness. RETRO/CARTOON?

Colour Palette

Our colour palette's primary colours are a light-warm beige, emphasizing the warmth and welcoming feel, and a bright blue with a warmer type of hue, connecting back to the original colours of the volleybal, but with a little twist to make our brand stand out more. Our secondary colours are a warm, retro type of yellow CHECKKK, tying back to the volleyball colours, and a retro red to make secondary elements like icons or lines pop out more.
